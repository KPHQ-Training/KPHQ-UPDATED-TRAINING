Welcome to your AI Operator path. Your program runs ten sessions across two weeks: eight hands-on training days, plus a Week 1 and a final assessment. The training days take you from AI foundations and Claude, through automation, GoHighLevel, and the tools you will use every day. Work through them in order. Each day opens as a self-contained interactive session, and your attendance is logged automatically when you begin. If you hit a term you do not know, the AI and GoHighLevel glossaries are linked at the bottom.
