Reference

Tools by Niche

The AI tools, automations, and compliance considerations most relevant to each client industry your VA may work in.

Legal
Law firms, solo practitioners, legal VAs
AI Tools
Claude ChatGPT Make.com GHL
Key Automations
  • Client intake form → CRM contact + onboarding sequence
  • Consultation booking reminders (24hr + 1hr)
  • Post-consultation follow-up with next steps
  • Retainer renewal reminders
  • Document request follow-up sequence
AI Best Use
  • Drafting client email templates and intake forms
  • Summarising internal meeting notes
  • Writing FAQ content and website copy
⚠ Compliance: Never input client names, case details, or privileged information into AI tools. Use AI for templates only — not case-specific content.
Healthcare
Clinics, private practices, health VAs
AI Tools
Claude Fathom Make.com GHL
Key Automations
  • Appointment reminders via SMS and email
  • No-show follow-up and rebooking sequence
  • Post-visit check-in message
  • Review request (after confirmed positive experience)
  • New patient welcome sequence
AI Best Use
  • Admin and front-desk email templates
  • Internal meeting notes (Fathom — staff meetings only)
  • Patient education content drafts for review
⚠ HIPAA: Never input patient names, DOB, diagnosis, symptoms, or any PHI into AI tools. AI is for admin and templates only — not patient-specific content.
Home Service
Cleaning, HVAC, plumbing, pest control
AI Tools
Claude Make.com GHL Zapier
Key Automations
  • Speed-to-lead: instant SMS when a new lead comes in
  • Estimate follow-up sequence (2 day, 5 day, 10 day)
  • Post-job review request
  • Missed-call text-back
  • Seasonal reactivation campaign
  • Referral request after 5-star review
AI Best Use
  • Writing estimate follow-up and review request copy
  • Before/after social content from job photos
  • Seasonal promotion emails and SMS scripts
⚠ Note: Always verify AI-generated pricing or scope language before sending to clients. Pricing is the VA's responsibility to confirm.
Real Estate
Agents, brokers, property managers
AI Tools
Claude Fathom Make.com GHL
Key Automations
  • New lead instant follow-up (speed-to-lead)
  • Open house follow-up sequence
  • Long-term buyer/seller nurture (30–90 day)
  • Listing update notifications to interested leads
  • Post-close referral request
AI Best Use
  • Listing descriptions and property copy
  • Buyer/seller email templates and follow-up scripts
  • Market update newsletter content
  • Meeting notes and action items from client calls
⚠ Note: Do not include undisclosed seller details, client financials, or offer amounts in AI prompts. Use anonymised placeholders.
Finance
Financial advisors, accountants, mortgage brokers
AI Tools
Claude Fathom Make.com GHL
Key Automations
  • New client onboarding sequence
  • Appointment scheduling and reminders
  • Quarterly check-in reminder sequences
  • Document request follow-ups
  • Annual review meeting booking campaign
AI Best Use
  • Client communication templates and newsletters
  • Meeting summary and action item drafts
  • Educational content and FAQ copy
⚠ Compliance: Never input client account numbers, SSNs, portfolio details, or financial data into AI tools. Regulatory compliance (SEC, FINRA) awareness required.
Contractors & Home Improvement
Remodelers, roofers, painters, landscapers
AI Tools
Claude Make.com GHL Zapier
Key Automations
  • Speed-to-lead SMS within 2 minutes of form submit
  • Estimate follow-up (3, 7, 14 day)
  • Project milestone update messages to homeowner
  • Post-completion review request
  • Referral request after 5-star review
  • Seasonal promotion campaign (spring/fall)
AI Best Use
  • Estimate cover letters and project proposals
  • Before/after social captions from job photos
  • Review request and referral scripts
  • Seasonal email and SMS campaign copy
⚠ Note: Always confirm scope and pricing language with the contractor before sending AI-drafted estimates or proposals to homeowners.
